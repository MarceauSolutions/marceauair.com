Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Marceau Air is a trade name of Marceau Solutions LLC ("we," "us"). This policy explains what information we collect when you use marceauair.com or request service, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

When you submit a service request we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, email (optional), service address, city, ZIP, the service you need, and any description you add. We also automatically record the date/time and IP address of your submission to document your consent.

How we use it

  • To contact you about your service request and schedule your appointment.
  • To dispatch a qualified, licensed HVAC technician to your address.
  • To keep records of the work and your communication preferences.

Text message (SMS) consent

By checking the consent box and submitting the form, you authorize Marceau Air and the technician matched to your request to contact you at the phone number you provide — by phone call, text/SMS, and email — about your service request. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can opt out of texts at any time by replying STOP, or call us to be removed. Reply HELP for help.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share your request details only with the licensed HVAC professional assigned to serve you, and with service providers that help us operate (e.g., messaging and scheduling tools), under confidentiality obligations. We may disclose information if required by law.

Your choices & rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and opt out of marketing communications, by contacting us below. Florida and other-state residents may have additional rights under applicable law.

Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide service and meet legal/record requirements, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
