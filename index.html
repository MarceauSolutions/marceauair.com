Common Questions

Things people actually ask us.

If your question isn't here, just call. We answer the phone.

How fast can you get to my house?
Most Naples, Bonita, and Estero appointments are scheduled within 24 hours. Same-day no-cool calls during peak season are handled in the order they come in — we'll give you an honest ETA, not a guess.
Do you charge a service or diagnostic fee?
We charge a flat diagnostic fee that's applied toward the repair if you move forward. You'll know the number before we leave the truck. No surprises.
Are you actually licensed and insured?
Yes — Florida State Certified Air Conditioning Contractor, Class A unlimited scope, license #CAC1814895. We carry general liability insurance on every job and can provide a Certificate of Insurance on request.
Do you offer financing on new systems?
For full system installations we partner with reputable financing options and will lay out the numbers transparently — including the total cost of borrowing — so you can compare paying cash vs. financing on the same page.
Do you do commercial work?
We're starting with residential to make sure every customer gets our full attention. Light commercial and property-management portfolios are case-by-case — give us a call.
What brands do you install?
We're brand-agnostic. We size and spec the system to your home, your duct layout, and your budget — not to whatever manufacturer is paying the highest spiff that month.
